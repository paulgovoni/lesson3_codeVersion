Moodologist

During Florida State Universities 30 hour hackathon, students from all around the east coast went from concept to prototype in one short weekend.

My team decided to work on a web app that we named "Moodologist" which would be your new personal bartender. Moodologist is an interactive webpage that generates an alcoholic beverage for you based on your mood. So instead of sitting at the bar not knowing what to order, Moodologist will give you the perfect drink you never knew you wanted in just a few clicks. The user is given the option to pick from 4 categories on the home page menu: emoji, landscape, words or colors. From the option chosen, the webpage will generate a drink that best fits your mood.

Each drink has been given a mood property based on the color of the drink, flavor (mixed with fruits, soda, etc.) and taste (sweet, sour, bitter) of the drink. The site utilizes HTML, CSS, Javascript, JQuery, and is built from scratch on the Bootstrap framework. The site was built within the 30 hour time limit of the competition.

At the end of the competition, our group was chosen as one of the final six to present our project in front of the entire hack fsu participants. We are happy to have gotten as far as we have!
